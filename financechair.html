Position: Finance Chair

Name: Vinh Nguyen

Email: v6nguyen@ucsd.edu

My name is Vinh Nguyen and I am the Finance Chair for Construction Management Association of America at UCSD. I am currently a senior standing and expect to graduate spring of 2017. I became interested in joining CMAA because it was another opportunity for me to expand my knowledge in this vast industry. I expect to learn the in’s and out’s of the construction processes, protocols, and time management skills that many of the industry workers have to deal with daily. CMAA at UCSD is not tailored to any specific major so I believe that this organization will be a great opportunity for students to come up with new ideas and innovative thinking.
