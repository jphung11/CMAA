Position: Co-President

Name: Gurunath Nesarajah

Email: gnesaraj@ucsd.edu

Gurunath Nesarajah is a 4th year at UCSD and is one of the Co-Presidents of CMAA at UCSD. He wants to bring more awareness to the field of Construction Management at UCSD by garnering interest in the CMIT program and reaching out to firms about giving guest lecturers and construction site tours for the students. Through CMAA, he hopes that many undergraduate students can become involved in the field of CM either during or after their undergraduate careers. In his free time, he likes to travel, eat out with friends and longboard.
