CMAA Student Membership Application Process

  1. Create an account on CMAA website.

    1. [CMAA Website]: https://cmaanet.force.com/CMAA_Self_Registration

      1. Begin Registration

  2. You will receive a link via email to create password and login to CMAA Membership Portal.

    1. [CMAA Membership Portal]: https://cmaanet.force.com/CMAA_Login_Page

  3. Select "Store" from the horizontal menu at the top of the page.

  4. Select "Membership" from the vertical menu on the left side of the page.

  5. Select “Individual Membership” - “Student”

  6. Click the "Add to Cart" button.

  7. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete your purchase.

    1. UCSD Transcript upload required! Download your UCSD Academic History as a PDF and submit it.
    2. Make payment of $25 for the Student Membership.
