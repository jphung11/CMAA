Faculty Advisor

Tara Hutchinson
Professor, Structural Engineering Department, UC San Diego

Professor Hutchinson is the faculty advisor for CMAA at UCSD. She is an earthquake engineering theoretician and experimentalist who has published significant papers in leading structural, civil, and earthquake engineering journals. In addition, she has creatively applied information technology to the evaluation of earthquake damage to structures, and her findings have been reported in publications that focus on computing, computer applications, instrumentation and measurement. Results from her soil-foundation structural analysis and seismic performance of non-structural building components have also helped in the design of these critical components.
