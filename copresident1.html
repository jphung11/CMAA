Hi! My name is Ni but I also go by Jenny, and I am the Co-President of CMAA at UCSD. I am currently a 4th year undergrad student at Jacob School of Engineering. I also work as a student drafter for the Space Planning and Facility Management Team on campus. In my free time, I practice Kendo, the Japanese-style Fencing, and I also enjoy cooking and going out on food adventures.

Early in year 2016, I have become interested in construction management. I am curious about the overall picture of a construction project and how it is planned and implemented from beginning to completion. With my Co-President partner Gurunath Nesarajah and the rest of the Officer Board, I decided to start a student chapter of CMAA here at UCSD, of which the objective is to offer opportunities to students who are interested in exploring this profession. I believe that through this organization, students interested in this field can learn more about it, build connections with the industry, plan for their career, achieve professional growth, and establish profound friendships with members.

Welcome and I look forward to the first year of CMAA at UCSD!
