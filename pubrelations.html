Position: Public Relations Chair

Name: Francisco Magana

Email: fjmagana@ucsd.edu

My name is Francisco Magana, I am senior at UCSD. Hobbies include cooking, weightlifting, and making playlists. I am the Public Relations Chair of CMAA at UCSD. I am interested in construction management because of its growing influence in major city areas and the lasting effects the industry has on our communities. By being a part of this organization I hope to make lasting connections within the industry and experience working with like-minded individuals. I am also a Construction Lead for UCSD's Steel Bridge Team. I hope to spread the word and grow this chapter to benefit individuals excited about construction management.
