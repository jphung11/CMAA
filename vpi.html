Position: Vice President Internal

Name: Jana Cadiz

Email: jccadiz@ucsd.edu

Hello friends! I am a 5th year undergraduate student at UCSD. My hobbies include basketball, volleyball, and piano. As the Vice President Internal of CMAA at UCSD, I assist our chapter’s Co-Presidents and collaborate with our chapter’s Vice President External. I also help maintain communication within the officer board and serve as the historian. I am primarily responsible for the logistics of meetings and the development of the chapter.

At an early age, I started volunteering and then interning with the City of Foster City’s Public Works Department, which manages infrastructure construction, operations and maintenance for streets, drainage, lagoon and levee, water and wastewater as well as the City’s Capital Improvement Program. While working with city engineers, contractors, planners, accountants, and residents, I was exposed to several projects that helped me learn and witness the changes and improvements within the community from the surveys and tasks I completed. The processes and impacts have led me to explore construction management.

With my involvement in CMAA at UCSD, I hope that the organization benefits from me as much I will benefit from it. I will be able to practice my leadership and management skills while giving back to the UCSD community. I hope to learn about and gain opportunities that I did not know were open to me. Furthermore, I would like build a network of both friendships and professional development.

Construction Management Association of America, UC San Diego Student Chapter
