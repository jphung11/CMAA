CMIT Program

Click here for the CMIT application.

The Construction Management In-Training Program is a process to attain a license to work in the construction management field and it is a comprehensive online program. Having this license makes you competitive in the construction field and teaches you hands on tip to deal with construction management. There is a mentor mentee system incorporated into the program when you finish the online process. This system assigns you a mentor who is a professional from the CMAA San Diego Chapter and he/she shows you the ropes within the construction management industry. Being a part of this system will also give you access to network with a plethora amount of professionals in construction field.
